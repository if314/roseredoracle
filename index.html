About

I am Kendall Eby and I offer gentle, intuitive tarot readings shaped by more than seven years of experience with the cards. My approach is warm, grounded, and transparent, creating a space where people feel safe, supported, and invited to explore their own inner wisdom. For events, I bring a softly decorated table and a curated selection of tarot decks for guests to choose from. Each person receives a 3 card intuitive reading, usually 5 to 10 minutes, offering clarity, comfort, and a moment of quiet magic. I take great care in crafting an atmosphere that feels calming and enchanting with subtle rose inspired touches, soft energy, and a presence that helps guests feel at ease.
