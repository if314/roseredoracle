FAQ

How long are sessions?

Single and 3-card readings are ~10–15 minutes. Moon Cycle and Celtic Cross are ~25–35 minutes.

Do you offer virtual readings?

Yes — indicate “virtual” in your notes and you’ll receive a secure video link.

How do I pay?

Cash, card, or mobile pay at the time of the session. Pre-pay links available upon request.
